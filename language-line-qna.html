Language Line - CSA Q&A

What is Language Line used for?

Language Line is used to help with telephone calls with customers who need information in their own language. This is done via a three-way phone call.

Do I need to arrange the conference call?

No. Language Line arranges the conference call facilities at their end. You do not need to do this yourself.

What should I tell the operator?

You should tell the operator whether the client is with you, or whether you need a dial-out. In a dial-out case, you stay on the line while the operator connects the translator and then the resident.
